Dynamo for Mathematica 4/5

We stopped updating the version of Dynamo for Mathematica 4/5 in April 2007 at version 0.1.5, but here are the workbooks just in case.

Download the Dynamo v0.1.5 workbooks here:

  • Dynamo 3S. For one-population, three strategy games.
  • Dynamo 4S. For one-population, four strategy games.
  • Dynamo 2x2. For two-population, 2 x 2 strategy games.
  • Dynamo 2x2x2. For three-population, 2 x 2 x 2 strategy games.

Please cite this group of workbooks as

W. H. Sandholm and E. Dokumaci (2007). Dynamo: Phase Diagrams for Evolutionary Dynamics, version 0.1.5. http://www.ssc.wisc.edu/~whs/dynamo.
